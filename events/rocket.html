Event Details

First Round Second Round Final Round

First Round

1. Participants (Student/Corporate) are allowed to participate in a team of two or three.

2. The 1st round will be an elimination round and it will be in the form of a marketing quiz.

3. Participants will have to answer 15 questions.

4. Time duration for the 1st round- 20 minutes.

5. Only one of the team member will be allowed to sit for the first round (Marketing Quiz).

6. Top 40 teams will qualify for the next round on the basis of their score.

Second Round

1. For the 2nd round, each team will be provided with a product, which they have to sell within a span of 45 minutes.

2. The qualification criteria will be based on the price margin and creativity with which the participants are able to sell it.

3. Top 10 teams will qualify for the final round.

Final Round

1. In the 3rd round, each qualifying team will be given various kinds of products and they have to bid for the given products.

2. There will be a minimum bidding price (base price) for a particular product and participants have to bid above that price and the one who bids the highest will have to sell that product with that bidding price being the base price.

3. Teams that will sell their product below their new bid price will be disqualified.

4. Winners will be decided based on the price margin between the base price and the price in which the product is sold in the least span of time.

5. Each team will be given a limited time to sell their product failing which they will be disqualified.

6. Participants able to sell the product in the least amount of time will have an advantage.

(Products for the event will be brought from KISS and the revenue generated from this event will be given to KISS)

7. Mobile phones are not allowed during the event.
